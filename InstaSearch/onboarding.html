How InstaSearch Works

Step 1: Installation

Install InstaSearch from our website or your browser's extension store.

Step 2: Activation

Click on the InstaSearch icon in your browser toolbar to activate it.

Step 3: Search

Highlight any text on a webpage and right-click to use InstaSearch.

Step 4: Results

View instant search results in a popup window without leaving the current page.

Step 5: Customize

Adjust settings to personalize your InstaSearch experience.

For more information, visit our Help Center.
